Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. What we collect

When you connect your TikTok account, MoneyPrinterBot receives an OAuth access token and the profile/video information TikTok grants under the scopes you approve (for example, basic profile info and Content Posting API access). We do not request your TikTok password.

2. How we use it

Access tokens are used solely to publish AI-generated videos to your TikTok account on your behalf, as configured by you. We do not sell your data or share it with third parties for advertising purposes.

3. Data storage

Access tokens and account identifiers are stored securely and only for as long as needed to operate the service for your account. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

4. Revoking access

You can disconnect MoneyPrinterBot from your TikTok account at any time via TikTok's app permissions settings, which immediately revokes our access.

5. Contact

Privacy questions or data-deletion requests can be sent to 66011232@kmitl.ac.th.
